Happy Diwali

Celebrating Diwali at Home

Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights, is a significant festival celebrated in India and other parts of the world. At home, it symbolizes the triumph of good over evil and light over darkness. Families clean and decorate their houses with rangolis, diyas (oil lamps), and fairy lights to welcome prosperity and good fortune.

Celebrating Diwali in College

In colleges, Diwali celebrations are an exciting time for students. Various cultural activities and competitions, such as rangoli contests, diya painting, and traditional dance performances, are organized. Students come together to decorate the campus, symbolizing unity and the spirit of togetherness.

Happy Diwali! May this festival bring joy and prosperity to all.
